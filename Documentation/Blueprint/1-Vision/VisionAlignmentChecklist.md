**GREEN LIGHT** ‚úÖ - Proceed with full confidence:

- [ ] Has clear success metrics
- [ ] Low risk, high impact

**YELLOW LIGHT** ‚ö†Ô∏è - Proceed with modifications:

**RED LIGHT** ‚ùå - Do not proceed:
